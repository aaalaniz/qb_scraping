In order to run logistic regression models we chose to binarize a small subset of our response variables. The cutoff of win percentage was 50%. The cutoff for completion percentage was 60%. The touchdown to interception ratio cutoff was 2:1. The passer rating cutoff was 80. Each one of these cutoffs represent a threshold of relative success.
